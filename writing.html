WRITING

A Dream

The light catches, creating millions of

imposter suns, sitting on the edge of deep

green spades bent down with the weight of

each dew drop.

They are about to fall, if

you wait a few seconds more they will spill

to the ground, warmed by the afternoon sun.

Religion

The tendons of his neck form a churches steeple

And is anything so holy as the view through a lovers eyes

A Letter to Annika

To me-

you are the smell of cinammon

and the spices you use while cooking,

the perfume you bought

from the witch you never found again.

You are crushed red velvet,

soft to the touch and warmed

by yellow-orange lamp light

that falls gently down

the slope of your nose and across your brow

you are the clinking of gold and silver rings

studded with multicolored gems and laughter

in short

to me-

you are home

Untitled

I found god in the warm lamp light

and heaven in your heartbeat

Model Town

The train rumbles and

the tiny plastic trees blow

stiffly in the wind


the lights shimmer

from the house windows

like tiny fires

the opening of soft green petals

spreading into a new form

black rot seaping through a termite eaten log

moonlit flesh

of thighs opened for a lover

waiting desperately for transformation

HM

i lie in my casket

and someone is playing soft piano

and people are talking about insignificant things.


i gaze at the cieling

and it turns into a swirling galaxy

of chipped paint and incandescent lighting
